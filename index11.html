We are agro-wizards

We provide information with graphical analysis about the crops in various counties. In addition to that we also depict the temperature conditions in those counties. We have started with 12 counties as of now with 14 crops. The data for analysis has been picked for a decade. Thus giving concrete statistics based on the stretch of data over 10 years.

  

Crop

Milk and Cream, Grapes, Lettuce, Almonds, Walnuts, Strawberries, Rice, Avacado, Tomatoes, Oranges, Cotton, Lemons, Potatoes, Pistachios and Carrots are worked on in various counties by us.

Climate

Different Crops are affected by the temperature, precipitation, snow, humidity and wind speed in a region. We gathered the statistics for these factors in the 12 counties of california.

County
